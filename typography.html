This was a design I made for my Typography class. The goal was to make a poster reminiscent of the Swiss style using only three colors. The poster had to be for an upcoming event at Oregon State University, and had to feature the name of the event, the date and location of where it was being held, and a small blurb about what the event entailed.

I chose two shades of green and white for my colors, and chose a design reminiscent of water droplets, as a reference to the name of the event 'Voices of the River'.
